SUMMER 2019 and OUTLIERS

The purpuse of this scatter plot is to take wind generation as a percent of total generation and correlate it with the day ahead and real time settlement of electricity prices in Texas. What I would expect to see is that if wind generation is high there is a less likely chance for energy prices to be high. And inversly on days where wind generation is low prices will more likely be high or even fall into "outlier" range. By including the date on the tooltip we can also correlate this information with expected load on these days by doing further analysis.
