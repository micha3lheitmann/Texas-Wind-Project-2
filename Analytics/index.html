An Analysis: Can We Predict Electricity Prices in Texas? - The answer is blowin' in the wind

Wind generation is increasing and along with it volatility and price outliers

The Future of Wind - Wind generation has increased rapidly in the last few years and expected to take another jump by 2025 with wind as a percent of total generation getting as high as 50%. Everyone agrees renewable generation is a good thing, however renewable generation is not responsive to changes in load and and creates a unique situation of price spikes, positive and negative along with overall volatility that is hard to predict. This will add a level of risk not seen in the market since the early 2000's when natural gas prices were trading at much higher levels.
